Technical Report

The product “DHARUNA” seems to have great values in Autonomous Industry and Traffic Management.A brief technical report is as follows:

TRAFFIC MANAGEMENT

Technically traffic flow has been divided into different classes “A to F” on the basis of comfort.When it comes to Class D the measuring of vehicle gets highly complicated and slight human error results to the collapse of traffic system.



In these stressful situations “Dharuna” stands as an aid to the driver.The app uses the GPS location to get the current coordinates of a vehicle and predicts the future coordinates using ML coded algorithm.When the Majority uses this app we have the current and future coordinates of traffic. By knowing the future traffic flow the TRAFFIC LIGHTS can be manipulated accordingly to avoid congestions. Consider a four phase signal system. After collecting the data from the transport department about the length of the queue formed every time the signal turns red and the time required to empty the queue. Taking the average of lengths as Lavg and taking the average of all the lengths that is less than Lavg as Lmin. Also calculate the average time required to empty the queue T. Since average length of car is 4.5meters the length of queue formed when N vehicles approaches the signal

L = 4.5N + min gap between two cars

Consider two cases:

CASE I: L > Lavg or Lavg < L < Lmin

Here we will set green time as average time i.e T. If L>Lavg all the vehicles within Lavg will cross and rest will have to wait. For Lavg>L>Lmin the vehicles will cross in time less than T and will allow extra vehicles to cross that will join during green time.

CASE II: L < Lavg

Here we will set the green time according to the time required to empty the length. Time required for Lavg is T So, Time requires for L is LTLavg = t So we will set green time as t and after adding an amber time we can turn the light red. In this way we will save time for others who are waiting for their signals to turn green.

Consider the scenario of a two lane or two way road where a vehicle wants to overtake another vehicle.



Now consider vehicle A wants to overtake vehicle B and vehicle C is travelling in the opposite direction. Speed of overtaking vehicle = Va m/s Speed of overtaken vehicle = Vb m/s Speed of opposing vehicle = Vc m/s The time between driver takes the decision and starts accelerating is considered as reaction time and for that time interval the vehicle A moves with the same velocity as vehicle B and try to maintain the distance with vehicle B and will travel distance d1 d1= Vb*t t = reaction time = 2.0sec At point A2 driver starts accelerating and overtakes the vehicle B d2=Vb*T+12*a*T2=b+2s T= Total time for required for overtaking manoeuvre (sec) Now b = Vb*T a = maximum overtaking acceleration (m/sec2) So 2s=aT22 , T=4s/a s =minimum safe distance s = 0.7(velocity of vehicle) + length of wheelbase During the manoeuvre the vehicle C moved the distance d3 with constant velocity d3=Vc*T Now if we add d1, d2, d3 we will get the Safe Overtaking distance i.e if a vehicle overtakes the min distance it will travel is Safe Overtaking distance.
